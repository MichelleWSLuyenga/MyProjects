About Us

Welcome to Pohs restaurant and food delivery service.
We are a family run restaurant that opened up in 1986 by our grandfather.
The restaurant has been open every day since 1986. We serve a blend of Asian cuisine,
Japanese, Vietnamese and Philippines food. Our delivery is open 24 hours a day
with great service and quick delivery times. Do not hesitate to contact us if you have any question.
